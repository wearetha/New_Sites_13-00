Meet Ceri

The passionate owner behind Vanity Fur

Vanity Fur was founded in 2022 by Ceri Garrity with one simple mission: to provide the very best care for dogs in Horwich and the surrounding Bolton area.

What started as a dream quickly became an award-winning reality. In April 2025, Ceri was honoured to win at the Bolton Women in Business Awards, a testament to the dedication and love poured into every furry guest.

With 81 glowing Google reviews and a 4.8-star rating, our customers consistently praise our caring environment, professional service and the genuine love we show every dog that walks through our doors.

Vanity Fur Pet Services Ltd is a registered limited company (Company No. 13699495), giving you the peace of mind that you're entrusting your best friend to a legitimate, professional business.
