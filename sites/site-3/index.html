About BL Joiners

BL Joiners is a trusted carpentry and joinery business with over 20 years of hands-on experience serving Horwich and the wider Bolton area. Our 5-star rating reflects our commitment to quality craftsmanship on every project.

From bespoke fitted kitchens and custom wardrobes to precision flooring and outdoor decking, we bring skill and attention to detail to every job. No project is too small — we treat every home as if it were our own.
