Meet Your Groomer

Horwich's Favourite Dog Groomer

A Personal, Friendly Service Run by Megan “Meg”

Pretty Little Pooch is a 5-star rated dog grooming salon nestled in the heart of Horwich. Run by Megan — known to all her regulars as Meg — the salon has built a reputation for gentle, caring grooming that keeps dogs (and their owners) coming back time and again.

With 28 five-star Google reviews and counting, Meg's personal, friendly approach means every dog is treated like her own. Customers consistently praise how their dogs love coming to the salon and always leave looking absolutely fantastic.

Whether it's a full groom, a puppy's first visit, or just a nail trim, your four-legged friend is in the safest of hands.
